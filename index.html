Our mission statement

Our project is dedicated to raising awareness and fostering understanding of the critical correlation between public health insurance access, affordable healthcare, and residential income disparities. Our goal is to provide insightful data and analysis that highlight how socioeconomic factors impact healthcare availability and overall community well-being. By promoting transparency and informed discussion, we aim to empower individuals, policymakers, and organizations to advocate for equitable healthcare solutions that serve all residents of New York City, regardless of income level or neighborhood.
